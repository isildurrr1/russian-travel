What didn't we see there?

According to VTsIOM polls, 95% of Russians dream of going somewhere, but only 36% plan to spend their holidays in their home country. Like, why are we here, at home, did not see? In fact, Russia is a whole universe with the gentle sea of the south, thick Sayan forests and severe ice of the Putorana plateau. And you can see all these beauties without millions in the account, passports and many hours of flights. Like, for example, Vera Bashmakova - a brave young mother, who took three children in an armful, seated them in her Lada and traveled 20 thousand kilometers in her native country. We have selected and described some interesting places worthy of your vacation.

Here, in the midst of forests and sand dunes, you can see two water horizons - the calm Curonian Lagoon on one side and the tucked rippling waves of the Baltic Sea on the other. Unique natural area on the edge Russian enclave.

The Kaliningrad region does not end there. Atmosphere of these places eliminates the fuss, dipping into the tranquility of nature and the smell of steel, cool sea.

Almost the entire peninsula is located beyond the Arctic Circle. Sami tundra, from which to the south - taiga, and to the north - the Arctic Ocean, pretending to be the Barents Sea.

Perhaps you watched Zvyagintsev and even heard the story of the Arctic festival in Teriberka. Perhaps the word "Khibiny" did not remain under the snow of school memories of geography lessons. But your dream of seeing the Northern Lights starts to come true with a ticket. to Murmansk.

Altai

URL

YouTube

Altai is one of the most beautiful places in Russia. First of all, because of the mountains: if you drive along the ridge, you will see slopes, strewn with pine trees, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open in the car windows, you can get acquainted with the invisible miracle of these places - mountain air.

The climate in Altai is temperate, so go here best in summer. So you will see all the variety of local flora and fauna. Moose roam the forests of Altai, eagles fly over the ridges, and on the plains deer graze. And the famous manuls are also inhabitants of the Altai Territory.

Everyone knows Baikal as the largest lake in the world. Many also know that this is the largest source of fresh water. water and one of the most beautiful places in Russia.

Of course, it's all true. But Baikal is still an ideal place for skiing competitions. This is a sport where the skier ties himself to the motorcycle, and the tandem tries to develop as much speed as possible on the ice. March 2019 at the festival "Baikal Mile" a world record was set - 197.011 km / h.

Karelia

URL

http://vodlozero.ru/

Siberia ends not in the Urals, but in Karelia: generatrix taiga, Siberian larch does not grow west of Vodlozero. But here she flaunts 30 meters - the forests of Karelian national parks due to impenetrable swamps never did not know the ax. Some pines are more than half a millennium old. Touch the living a creature that saw the sun before Ivan the Terrible saw it. In the virgin forest for a hundred kilometers you will not find a path. And on rare paths trees a couple of meters from the ground are marked with bear claws. Let everyone know who's boss.
